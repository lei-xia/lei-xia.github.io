About Me

I am a R&D engineer working on big data infrastructure in LinkedIn. I got my Ph.D degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Northwestern University in June 2013. During my Ph.D adventure in Northwestern, I worked in the Prescience Lab with Prof. Peter A. Dinda.

Research Interest

Projects

  • VNET/P: High Speed/Low Latency Virtual Overlay Network to bridge Cloud and High Performance Computing
  • Virtual WiFi: An virtualization approach for wireless network devices (Intern project at Intel Labs during Summer 2010)
